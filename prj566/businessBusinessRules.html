BR#
Business Rule
Use Case
1 Only projects approved by appropriate instructor can become available for PRJ566 students. UC07, UC10
2 Company representative and Team Leader accounts can only be created by the appropriate instructor. UC09, UC11
3 When particular semester is over, the appropriate instructor must proceed the matched projects to appropriate semester UC12
4 All team and project ranking must be done before the appropriate instructor matches them. UC02, UC18
5 In case there are more projects available then teams, team leaders must rank the same amount of project as there are teams in the particular semester. UC18
6 Each team/project must be ranked with a different ranking number (sequentially). UC02, UC18
7 Appropriate instructor must be notified when a new project is registered. UC07, UC10
8 Before a company representative can register a company and a new project, he/she must acknowledge the Legal Agreement Contract. UC07
9 Company page must be created during the company account creation (generated from a template). UC06, UC09
10 Team Page must be created from a template. UC05, UC19
11 Team Leader must specify the team constraints when publishing the page. UC05, UC19
12 Appropriate instructor should be able to edit the matching results and all changes must be tracked. UC13
13 Company representative should be able to check project status. UC03
14 Project status must be up to date and updated by a team leader. UC03
15 When teams are matched with the projects all parties must be notified. UC13
16 Each project must have milestones with the appropriate status.
17 All created accounts must have a temporary password generated by the system, and it must be changed on the first log in. UC09, UC11, UC16, UC19, UC21
18 Company representative should be able to leave comments upon project completion. UC08, UC23
19 Site administrator must be notified when a comment is left. UC08
20 Company comments can be viewed by site visitors only after appointed site administrator approval. UC08, UC23
21 Appropriate Instructor should be able to notify all parties when it is time to do ranking. UC02, UC18
22 When a particular semester is over, the appropriate instructor must deactivate the current team accounts UC15
23 Visitors should be able to view all projects upon completion as past projects.
24 Team leader should be able to customize project milestones. UC17
25 Project files can only be viewed by the company representative and the team matched to their project. UC01
26 Instructor and faculty accounts can only be managed by appointed site administrator. UC21
27 Only valid Seneca e-mail addresses are accepted for Instructor accounts. UC21
28 Team Leaders and Company Representatives should be able to do ranking more then once (overriding the old results).
29 All Team member e-mails must be Seneca learn e-mails UC19, UC20
30 Faculty should be able to view all the projects that match his/her identifier
31 Faculty should be able to change the project status to Past UC20
32 Instructor account must be notified by the system when deactivated. UC21
33 Only appointed site administrator can manage site accounts UC21
34 If necessary, the appointed site administrator should be able to change appropriate instructor and faculty name and e-mail before semester starts.
